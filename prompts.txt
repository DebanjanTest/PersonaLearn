MASTER PROJECT SPECIFICATION PERSONALEARN

THE VISION
PersonaLearn is a Fluid Intelligence Platform that breaks the rigid silos of traditional education tech. It recognizes that a user can be a student a teacher and a professional all in one day. The interface and AI context shift instantly based on the active Persona transforming from a study companion to an administrative assistant or a corporate productivity tool.

STUDENT PERSONA
The Student view creates a self contained learning environment. The Dashboard drives motivation with Study Streaks and Weekly Goals while AI analyzes activity to suggest specific Improvement Areas. The suite of AI Study Tools transforms passive reading into active engagement. Immersive Flashcards feature 3D animations for focused review. The Smart QA Generator creates structured tests including Fill in the Blanks and MCQs rendered in interactive cards rather than raw text. The Group Project Tracker analyzes chat logs to visualize individual contributions and solve credit disputes. The Citation Generator provides instant APA and MLA formatting. Visual Mind Maps turn complex text into clear hierarchical diagrams. The Exam Simulator allows students to upload content generate custom exams take them in a realistic environment and receive immediate AI grading with granular feedback. The Code Playground offers a safe simulation environment for JavaScript Python C plus plus and HTML.

TEACHER PERSONA
The Teacher view reduces administrative burden. The Advanced Exam Creator generates professional print ready exam papers complete with school branding and precise control over question distribution. The Classroom tool serves as a digital attendance register allowing teachers to log presence and behavioral notes efficiently. Performance Analytics transform raw grade sheets into actionable insights identifying at risk students and visualizing class performance trends on a bell curve.

PROFESSIONAL PERSONA
Designed for workplace efficiency this persona includes a Financial Health Widget to visualize income allocation and a Burnout Barometer to monitor work hours. The Meeting Cost Calculator tracks the real time financial impact of meetings. The Communication Lab offers tools like a Slang Translator to decode corporate jargon a LinkedIn Ghostwriter for personal branding and an Email Polisher to ensure diplomatic communication.

BUSINESS PERSONA
For entrepreneurs the Strategy Center generates SWOT and PESTLE analyses. The Pitch Deck Generator creates slide outlines with visual suggestions. The Brand Kit Generator offers industry specific color palettes and font pairings. The Competitor Spy leverages Google Search to provide real time pricing intelligence.

INTERVIEW PREP PERSONA
Job seekers benefit from a Resume Scanner that scores CVs against job descriptions. The Mock Interview Simulator conducts adaptive role specific interviews with instant feedback. The Offer Letter Decoder scans contracts to highlight potential legal red flags.

ASPIRANT PERSONA
Competitive exam candidates access a Daily Digest of relevant news. The Trend Probability tool predicts topic relevance based on past patterns while the Mnemonic Generator creates memory aids for complex facts.

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
The platform runs on a responsive multilingual framework supporting English Hindi Bengali Punjabi Marathi and Telugu. It utilizes Hybrid Storage with IndexedDB and LocalStorage for a persistent app like feel. Powered by Gemini 2 5 Flash it delivers fast multimodal intelligence across all personas with dynamic theming that adapts the visual mood to the user's current role.
